• Fill in the numbers or letter in the board (Depends on the difficulty you choose):
    • For EASY: Fill the numbers from 1 to 4.
    • For MEDIUM: Fill the numbers from 1 to 9.
    • For HARD: Fill the numbers from 1 to 9 and letter from A to G.
  • In each row and column of the board, each number and letter can appear only once.
  • Within each 'large cell' (e.g., 2x2 for EASY, 3x3 for MEDIUM, 4x4 for HARD), each number and letter can appear only once.
  • You win if you complete the board while satisfying all the above conditions.
